Web이란 무엇인가?

he World Wide Web (WWW), commonly known as the Web, is an information system that enables information sharing over the Internet through user-friendly ways meant to appeal to users beyond IT specialists and hobbyists.[1] It allows documents and other web resources to be accessed over the Internet according to specific rules of the Hypertext Transfer Protocol (HTTP).[

It is often assisted by technologies such as Cascading Style Sheets (CSS) and scripting languages such as JavaScript.
